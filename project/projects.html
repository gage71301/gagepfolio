Duck Quiz App

LINK TO LIVE APP GITHUB REPO

Desecription

Simplistic Quiz App about ducks with 5 multiple-choice questions. The user can answer the questions and the quiz will grade their answers and show them whether they got the right or wrong answer. At the end of the quiz, the user will be prompted with a retry button that will reset the score and restart the quiz from the beginning.
